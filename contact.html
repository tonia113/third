courage for the gospel

Have not I commanded thee? Be strong and of good courage; be not afraid, neither be thou dismayed: for the LORD thy God is with thee whithersoever thou goest.(Joshua 1:9)

Courage is the ability and will to do or stand for what is right or neccessary even in the face of adversity or opposition.Its the ability to face danger and oppsition fearlessly and affirm your faith in Christ no matter the adversaries that you face.
